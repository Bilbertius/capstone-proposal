As a user I will be able to...

  ...create playlists of live recordings where each song is defined with the date and venue of the recording, plus tags 
  regarding the theme of the imporovisational sections and any other noteworthy elements as well as information regarding
  where to listen to said recording. 
  
  ...update playlists by adding and removing songs.
  
  ...update songs with any relevant tags that may help further distiguish a particular live rendition.
  
  ...delete songs from playlists or entire playlists.
  
  ...read playlists I have created by viewing my profile. 
  
  ...read playlists created by other users. 
  
  ...update the rating of playlists created by other users by casting a vote that will be used in calculating the average rating.
